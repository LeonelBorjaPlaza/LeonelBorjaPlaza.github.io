Hello! I'm a Ph.D. candidate in the Dyson School of Applied Economics and Management at Cornell University. I am interested in development economics, applied econometrics, and labor economics. My research is often motivated by fairness and equality of opportunity, but I'm interested in most things that can advance economic development. My current work explores the intergenerational transmission of human capital and its implications for equity of opportunity.
